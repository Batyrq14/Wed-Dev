Let's talk about cities

Almaty

Location : Located in the southeastern part of Kazakhstan.
Significance: Former capital of Kazakhstan until 1997, it remains the country's largest city and a major cultural and economic hub.
Features: Surrounded by the stunning Tien Shan mountain range, Almaty is known for its scenic beauty, parks, and vibrant cultural scene.

Astana

Location: Situated in the northern part of Kazakhstan.
Significance: Designated as the capital of Kazakhstan in 1997, later renamed Nur-Sultan in 2019 in honor of Nursultan Nazarbayev, the country's first President.
Features: Known for its modern architecture, including the Bayterek Tower and the Khan Shatyr Entertainment Center. Nur-Sultan serves as the political and administrative center of Kazakhstan.

Shymkent

Location: Located in the southern part of Kazakhstan.
Significance: Third-largest city in the country and an important industrial and cultural center.
Features: Rich in history and culture, Shymkent has various historical sites, parks, and museums. It serves as a key transportation hub in the region.
